Cells & Committees

Various committees working for smooth governance and student welfare.

Academic Council

Responsible for academic planning, syllabus development, and maintaining educational standards.

Finance Committee

Manages budgeting, financial planning, and resource allocation.

Curriculum Framing cum Planning Committee

Designs and updates curriculum according to educational guidelines.

Library Committee

Oversees library management, acquisitions, and digital resources.

Sexual Harassment Committee

Ensures a safe campus environment by addressing harassment complaints.

Grievance Redressal Committee

Handles complaints and ensures fair resolutions.

Planning and Evaluation Committee

Evaluates institutional progress and strategic planning.

Examination Committee

Conducts examinations and ensures fair assessment.

Admission Committee

Manages student admissions and enrollment process.

Student Welfare Committee

Supports student well-being and extra-curricular activities.

SC/ST/OBC Cell

Ensures representation and welfare of SC/ST/OBC students.

Minority Cell

Safeguards the interests of minority communities.

Cultural Sub-Committee

Organizes cultural programs, competitions, and events.
