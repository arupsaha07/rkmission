About the Library (KOHA)

Our library is digitally empowered using KOHA open-source LMS. It manages cataloging, circulation, and OPAC. The system ensures efficient access to books and journals, making the library a true knowledge hub.

Library Committee

The committee is responsible for managing the procurement, cataloging, and expansion of library resources. It includes the Principal, senior faculty members, and the librarian to ensure policy transparency and continuous improvement.

Library Rules

Silence must be maintained at all times inside the library.

Return books on time to avoid late fines.

Do not share your library card with others.

No food or drinks allowed inside the library.

Switch off mobile phones or keep them silent.

Handle books and materials with care to avoid damage.
