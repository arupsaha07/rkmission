Overview of the Bachelor of Education program

Eligibility

Who can apply for B.Ed

Candidates must have completed their undergraduate degree with the minimum percentage as prescribed by the affiliating university. Specific subject combinations, reservation norms, and other criteria apply as per institutional regulations. All documents must be submitted for verification during admission. Misrepresentation may lead to cancellation.

Rules and Regulations

Admission-related policies

Applicants are required to follow the admission guidelines, submit accurate documents, and abide by ethical conduct throughout the process. Misrepresentation may lead to cancellation.

Rules and Criteria

Selection & evaluation guidelines

Merit is calculated based on the qualifying examination scores and reservation norms. Candidates must submit all documents for verification. Tie-breaker rules apply as per institutional policy.
