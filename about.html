Our College

Sree Sree Ramkrishna B.Ed College is located in a serene rural area that encourages learning in a pollution-free, nature-rich environment. Established in 2006, the college is committed to shaping well-rounded educators with strong values and modern pedagogical skills.

Our vision is to provide an inclusive and progressive educational atmosphere that nurtures creativity, critical thinking, and professional growth.

Academic Calendar

The academic session is divided into two semesters, each approximately 20 weeks long: Autumn Semester (July-November) and Spring Semester (January-April).

The Senate-approved academic schedule includes registration, mid-term and end-semester exams, breaks, and holidays. It is published on the Institute website each year. Each semester aims to have around 90 working days to ensure academic rigor and consistency.

Code of Conduct

Discipline

All students are expected to maintain discipline and decorum within the campus and during academic sessions. Violations may lead to disciplinary action.

Attendance

Minimum 75% attendance is mandatory for each subject. Failing to meet this requirement will result in disqualification from final exams.

Academic Honesty

Cheating, plagiarism, or other academic misconduct is strictly prohibited and will lead to disciplinary measures as per college regulations.

Respect & Ethics

Every student is expected to show respect to peers, faculty, and staff, and uphold high ethical standards inside and outside the classroom.

Dress Code

Students must adhere to the prescribed dress code and maintain cleanliness and hygiene as a part of their professional identity.

Mobile Usage

Use of mobile phones or electronic gadgets in classrooms is restricted unless approved by faculty for academic purposes.
